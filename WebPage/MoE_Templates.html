Methods of Engagement

The NaaS PlayBook is the vehicle to serve as the planning and implementation guide for RST NaaS initiatives.


Templates

The templates are files that are created with an overall layout to be used with one or more documents. These templates help to accomplish, organize and request tasks that are required in the modules. Templates vary depending on the kind of work but should share similar themes and patterns throughout the completed work.
